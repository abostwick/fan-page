What is Overwatch?

Overwatch is a First Person shooter done in a MOBA style, where you select heros with different abilities that accomplish different agendas. It's a game that breaks away from the modern military shooter genre where it had to worry about realism and what the real weapons and feel of real world war felt like. With Overwatch, you get a fun, colorfull shooter that can do or try anything it puts its mind to, and with all the different heros and abilities, it's like a mash up of many genres into one game.

Roles in Overwatch

  • Offense, which have the highest DPS (damage per second) out of all the heros, but have low health.
  • Defense, which are capable of fortifying positions to stop enemy advances.
  • Tank, where you are practically a bullet sponge that can break through enemy lines and defenses.
  • Support, such as healing, buffs, debuffs, and revives.
